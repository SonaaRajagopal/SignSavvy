About Us

What is a Traffic Signal Detector?
A traffic signal detector is an innovative device used to monitor and control traffic signals at intersections.
These detectors utilize various technologies, including radar, infrared sensors, video cameras, and induction loops, to detect the presence and movement of vehicles, cyclists, and pedestrians
